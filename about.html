A Near Future  ·  Detroit

About.

"A Near Future isn't just about showcasing creativity, it's about impacting the future. A future that's already very near."

A Near Future is a Detroit-based digital magazine and media platform dedicated to spotlighting young people who are building - entrepreneurs, creatives, artists, musicians, scientists, and community leaders who are creating before the world gives them permission to.

We believe the stories of young builders deserve the same editorial weight and design quality as the publications that have historically overlooked them. Every feature, article, and podcast episode is built to reflect the seriousness of what these young people are doing.

A Near Future is not a social media page with a logo. It is a publication - with editorial standards, a visual identity, and a commitment to telling these stories with the depth they deserve.

Our Mission

01

Spotlight the Unseen

Find and feature young people who are building something real - before the mainstream notices. Be the publication that gets there first and tells the story right.

02

Elevate the Standard

Every piece of content - from the photography to the copywriting to the design - should reflect the quality of the people we cover. No shortcuts. High standard, always.

03

Build Community

Connect young builders across Detroit and beyond. Create a network of young people who find each other through this platform and build together because of it.

Donald Williams Jr.

Founder's Note

"I built A Near Future because I wanted to see the people around me reflected in something that took them seriously."

Growing up in Detroit, I saw extraordinary young people doing extraordinary things with no spotlight on them. No platform treating their work with the weight it deserved. I started A Near Future to change that.

This isn't a side project. This is a publication. And we're going to build it the same way we ask the people we feature to build - with intention, with patience, and with a near future in mind.

Every story we tell is a story that deserves to be told. Every person we feature is someone who earned it. That standard doesn't change.

Donald Williams Jr.

Founder & Editor, A Near Future

Nonprofit Arm

A Near Future Foundation

The nonprofit arm of A Near Future supports young builders directly - through grants, mentorship programming, and educational resources designed to remove the barriers between young people and their near futures.

We partner with Detroit-area organizations, businesses, and foundations to connect emerging leaders with the tools, networks, and funding they need to go from idea to impact.
