A Near Future

Nominate.

Know a young person who deserves to be seen? Tell us who they are and why they belong in a near future.

Who we feature

Entrepreneurs, creatives, artists, musicians, scientists, builders, and community leaders ages 13 to 25.

Where we look

Detroit and Metro Detroit first - but we cover the next generation wherever they're building.

What we look for

Real work. Real stories. People who are already building - not waiting for permission. If they're doing it before anyone is watching, we want to tell that story.

What happens next

We review every submission. If your nominee is selected, we'll reach out directly to set up an interview and feature.

The next generation of leaders, builders, and visionaries - a near future.
